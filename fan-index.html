Who are they

Phantom Regiment is a drum cops based in Rockford, Illinois. It was founded by Alex Haddad in 1956. Before the founding of DCI (Drum Corps International) Phantom Regiment was mostly performing locally. Phantom Regiment tied with the Blue Devils in the first DCI.

Why are they a good drum corps

Phantom Regiment has had many amazing shows from Spartacus in 2008 to Mynd in 2024. In 2008 when they performed Spartacus they beat the Blue Devils taking first place in that years DCI. Spartacus was a show about the gladiator Spartacus who led a slave revolt in the Roman empire
